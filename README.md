## Featured Projects

**1. A/B Testing for Product Decisions (ABtest)**
A complete A/B testing pipeline that evaluates product changes using **statistical rigor** rather than intuition. The project covers **experiment design, metric definition, hypothesis testing, and lift analysis** to quantify impact on key KPIs. It demonstrates how I translate experimental results into **actionable recommendations** for product and business stakeholders.

**2. Customer Churn Prediction (Churn-Prediction)**
An end-to-end churn prediction project that builds, evaluates, and interprets models to **identify at-risk customers** before they leave. It includes **data preprocessing, feature engineering, model comparison, and business-oriented threshold setting**. The focus is on how to use churn scores to prioritize retention efforts and estimate potential revenue impact.

**3. HR Analytics & Dashboards (HR_Analytics & Tableau)**
A portfolio of HR analytics and BI work combining **Python, SQL, and Tableau**. These repositories include dashboards and analyses around workforce metrics such as **attrition, performance, and engagement**. They highlight my ability to design **stakeholder-friendly dashboards**, define the right HR KPIs, and communicate insights clearly to non-technical audiences.

**4. RAG Chatbot with Groq (rag-chatbot-groq)**
A retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) chatbot that combines **LLMs with domain-specific context** over vector search to answer questions accurately and efficiently. The project covers **data ingestion, embedding generation, retrieval, and LLM orchestration**, along with deployment instructions. It demonstrates how I apply **GenAI to real documentation and knowledge bases**.

**5. Clinical Trial Outcome Prediction (medical-project-Pharma)**
A machine learning project focused on predicting clinical trial outcomes using structured medical and trial data. It includes **feature engineering, model selection, and evaluation** with metrics such as accuracy and AUC-ROC (achieving **85%+ accuracy and 94.6% AUC-ROC** in experiments). The emphasis is on **high-stakes classification, careful validation, and interpretability** for domain experts.

**6. RL-Driven EV Charging Optimization (RL_Electric_Vehicle_Charging)**
A reinforcement learning project that optimizes EV charging schedules to **reduce peak load and energy costs**. Using RL agents, the system learns to shift demand and achieved **15-25% peak reduction** under tested scenarios. This project shows how I apply **advanced ML to real-world operational and energy problems** with clear, quantitative impact.
